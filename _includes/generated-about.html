Fabio Giglietto, PhD, is an esteemed Associate Professor of Internet Studies within the Department of Communication Sciences, Humanities and International Studies at the University of Urbino Carlo Bo. He brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise in the fields of information theory, communication, and the intricate dynamics between social systems, media, and digital technologies. His work delves deeply into the societal impacts of digital communication, positioning him as a thought leader in understanding how media and technology shape public discourse and behavior.

Professor Giglietto's prolific research has been widely published in leading academic journals, including the Journal of Communication, Information, Communication & Society, and Social Media + Society. Among his notable works are studies on second screen participation, the role of social media platforms like Facebook and Twitter in research, and the dissemination of disinformation. His acclaimed publication, "Second Screen and Participation: A Content Analysis on a Full Season Dataset of Tweets," has garnered significant attention with 397 citations, reflecting his research's broad impact and relevance.

Currently, Professor Giglietto is deeply engaged in projects that explore the intersection of social media analysis and disinformation. His recent research activities focus on the computational analysis of social behavior and media manipulation, particularly within the contexts of political communication and elections. Engaging with a wider academic and professional community, he actively shares insights on platforms such as BlueSky and Mastodon, where his discussions often highlight the challenges and opportunities presented by digital media in contemporary society.

In addition to his academic pursuits, Professor Giglietto is involved in collaborative projects that seek to advance the understanding of digital information ecosystems. His contributions have been pivotal in securing research grants aimed at exploring innovative methodologies in social sciences. With a career marked by continuous inquiry and impactful scholarship, Fabio Giglietto remains at the forefront of internet studies, continually inspiring both his peers and students through his dedication to advancing knowledge in this rapidly evolving field.
